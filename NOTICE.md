# Data attribution and licensing

The raster products generated by this project are derived from
**Copernicus Sentinel data** acquired and processed by the
**European Space Agency (ESA)**, delivered through the
**Microsoft Planetary Computer**.

## Sentinel-2 data

Copernicus Sentinel data is provided free of charge under the
[Copernicus Sentinel Data products terms and conditions](https://sentinel.esa.int/documents/247904/690755/Sentinel_Data_Legal_Notice).

When publishing or redistributing derived products, include the following
attribution:

> Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel data 2026, processed by ESA.

## Microsoft Planetary Computer

The data is distributed by Microsoft under the
[Community Data License Agreement – Permissive (CDLA-1.0)](https://cdla.dev/permissive-1-0/).
